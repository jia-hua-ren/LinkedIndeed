# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 3, 2026

## Overview

This browser extension is designed to operate entirely on your device. I respect your privacy and do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

## Information Collection

This extension does **not** collect any of the following:

* Personal information
* Browsing history
* Account information
* Device information
* Location data
* Usage analytics
* Cookies or tracking data

## Data Processing

Any data processed by the extension is handled locally on your device and is used solely to provide the extension's functionality. No data is transmitted to external servers controlled by the developer.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services.

## Data Storage

If the extension stores settings or preferences, they are stored locally within your browser and remain under your control.

## Data Sharing

Because no user data is collected, no user data is shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to any third parties.

## Security

Since the extension operates locally and does not transmit user information, there is no collection or storage of personal data by the developer.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the extension. Any updates will be posted in this repository.

# Extension permissions specifics

## activeTab justification
Needs to look at your current tab, which is the *specific* job post page, to extract information from it.

## storage justification
Stores your Excel/Google Sheets settings so that when you click "copy excel", it puts the relevant information together into a row for you to copy and paste.

## clipboardWrite justification
Used for the "Copy Excel" button which puts the relevant information together from the job post into a row for you to copy.

## Host permission justification
Needs to be able to identify which website the extension is on. If it is not on a supported website, the extension will do nothing. If it is on the home page of a job board site, but not a specific job posting page, it will prompt the user to open up a job post on a new tab.
